Course Enrollment & Grade Entry

Procedure: InsertGrade

Simulates database triggers (Prerequisite Enforcements & Course Limits) and the grade automation procedure.

Active Database Triggers

These triggers are validated programmatically during execution:

trg_CheckCourseEnrollmentLimit BEFORE INSERT

Ensures that **no more than 20 students** can be enrolled in any individual course code. Attempts to add more will abort the transaction.

trg_EnforcePrereqCompletion BEFORE INSERT

Verifies that a student has completed and passed (status = 'PASS') all required prerequisites for a course before allowing enrollment.
